Who We Are

A Complete One-Stop
Manufacturing Solution

Since 1984, Teck See Plastic has grown to become a leading plastic injection contract manufacturer — offering a comprehensive range of services from design and prototyping through to moulding, assembly and marketing, serving industries across Malaysia and the Asia-Pacific region.

As a wholly owned subsidiary of Oriental Holdings Berhad, TSP operates six integrated companies, each specialising in a distinct stage of the plastic manufacturing value chain — delivering full accountability under one group.
