Company Overview

Compounding & Colouring
Sdn Bhd (CCSB)

Established in April 1994 as a subsidiary of Teck See Plastic Group under Oriental Holdings Berhad, CCSB was set up with technical assistance from Mitsubishi Chemical / Japan Polychem Corporation for the Proton project.

With 90% of its product range manufactured from polypropylene (PP), CCSB supplies precision-formulated compounds across interior, exterior, HVAC and engine parts. The company's top three products are trim parts, bumper parts and door trims — supplying directly to Perodua, Proton and Toyota platforms.

Mission
"To be a global manufacturing company, where contribution towards INNOVATION and excellent in quality, on time cost, flexibility, engineering and customer service is our priority."
