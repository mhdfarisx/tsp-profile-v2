CCSB — Business Milestones

30 Years of Continuous Supply

From compound supplier to a full-service automotive partner — 1994 to present.

TSP Group › CCSB › History
1994
CCSB Established
Founded under Teck See Group / Oriental Holdings Berhad with technical assistance from Mitsubishi Chemical and Japan Polychem Corporation. Set up to supply customised polymer compounds for Malaysia's growing automotive industry under the Proton programme.
Key Milestone
1995
First Automotive Supply — Proton Wira & Iswara Bumper
CCSB's first commercial compound supply to Hicom Teck See for the Proton Wira and Iswara bumper programme.
Customer: Hicom Teck See
2001
Perodua Kelisa Bumper
Expanded into the Perodua supply chain through the Kelisa bumper programme with Ace Polymer — broadening CCSB's customer base beyond Proton.
Customer: Ace Polymer
2004
Proton Waja Bumper
Secured compound supply for the Proton Waja bumper programme, reinforcing CCSB's position within the Hicom Teck See supply chain.
Customer: Hicom Teck See
2005
Perodua Myvi Door Trim
First door trim compound programme — marking CCSB's expansion from exterior bumper compounds into interior trim applications.
Customer: Hicom Teck See
2006
Proton Satria IP & Naza Ria Bumper
Added instrument panel (IP) compound to CCSB's product range through the Proton Satria programme with AHP, alongside bumper supply for the Naza Ria via TSP (SA). First IP programme milestone.
Customers: AHP / TSP (SA)
2007
Perodua MYVI FL & VIVA SE — Bumper & Door Trim
Compound supply for Perodua MYVI FL bumper and door trim, and Perodua VIVA SE bumper programmes through HTS and Permintex.
Customers: HTS / Permintex
2009
Perodua Nautica Bumper & D20N IP & Child Part
Perodua Nautica bumper via Hicom Teck See, and IP & child part programmes for Perodua D20N through Permintex — expanding IP compound capabilities further into the Perodua platform.
Customers: Hicom Teck See / Permintex
2010
Perodua Alza Bumper & Proton Exora Bumper
Multi-platform, multi-customer expansion — Perodua Alza via Hicom Teck See and Proton Exora bumper through TSP (SA).
Customers: Hicom Teck See / TSP (SA)
2012
Proton Preve Door & Pillar Trim; Toyota Vios Bumper Grille
Door and pillar trim for the Proton Preve via Delloyd, and bumper grille for the Toyota Vios via AHP — CCSB's first supply into the Japanese automotive segment.
Customers: Delloyd / AHP
2013
Proton Iriz Door & Pillar Trim
Door and pillar trim compound supply for the Proton Iriz via Delloyd — continued growth in the interior trim segment.
Customer: Delloyd
2015
Proton New Perdana Bumper & Door Trim; Proton Persona & Saga Door Trim
Bumper and door trim for the Proton New Perdana via KTSM, and door trim for Proton Persona and Saga through Delloyd — multiple Proton platforms served simultaneously.
Customers: KTSM / Delloyd
2016
Perodua Bezza Door Trim & IP Child Part
Door trim compound for Perodua Bezza via KTSM, and IP child part compound through Permintex — covering both interior trim and instrument panel applications.
Customers: KTSM / Permintex
2017
Perodua D20N Bumper RR & D38L Door Trim
Rear bumper compound for Perodua D20N via PDKM, and door trim compound for D38L via KTSM — ahead of the D38L full launch in 2018.
Customers: PDKM / KTSM
2018
Perodua Aruz (D38L) Bumper FR/RR — IATF 16949:2016 Certified
Front and rear bumper compound for the Perodua Aruz via PDKM. Milestone year: CCSB achieved IATF 16949:2016 certification — the automotive industry's premier quality management system standard.
IATF 16949:2016 Certified
2020
Perodua D55L — Bumper RR/IP & Interior Trim
CCSB's most comprehensive single-platform programme to date — covering exterior, IP, and interior trim simultaneously for the Perodua D55L.
Customers: PDKM / Hil / AHP
2021
Perodua D51A — Bumper Front & IP
Front bumper and instrument panel compound supply for the Perodua D51A platform via PDKM.
Customer: PDKM
2022
Perodua Alza 2022 (D27A) — Bumper Front & IP
Front bumper and IP compound for the all-new Perodua Alza 2022 — the highest-volume Perodua MPV launch of the decade.
Customer: PDKM
2023
Perodua Axia 2023 (D74A) — Bumper, IP & Door Trim
Full-platform compound supply for the new Perodua Axia — front bumper and IP via PDKM, door trim via KTSM. One of CCSB's broadest single-model coverages.
Customers: PDKM / KTSM
2025
Perodua D66B — Bumper Front, IP & Door Trim
Latest Perodua D66B platform — front bumper, IP and door trim via PDKM and KTSM. Ongoing active programme as of 2025.
Customers: PDKM / KTSM
