CCSB — Business Milestones

30 Years of Continuous Supply

From compound supplier to a full-service automotive partner — 1994 to present.

TSP Group › CCSB › History
1994
CCSB Established
⌄
Founded under Teck See Group / Oriental Holdings Berhad with technical assistance from Mitsubishi Chemical and Japan Polychem Corporation. Established to supply customised polymer compounds for the growing Malaysian automotive industry under the Proton programme.
Key Milestone
1995
First Automotive Supply — Proton Wira & Iswara Bumper
⌄
CCSB's first commercial compound supply to Hicom Teck See for the Proton Wira and Iswara bumper programme — marking CCSB's entry into the national automotive supply chain.
Customer: Hicom Teck See
2001
Perodua Kelisa Bumper
⌄
Expanded into the Perodua supply chain through the Kelisa bumper programme with Ace Polymer — broadening CCSB's customer base beyond Proton.
Customer: Ace Polymer
2004
Proton Waja Bumper
⌄
Secured compound supply for the Proton Waja bumper programme, reinforcing CCSB's position as a trusted compound supplier within the Hicom Teck See supply chain.
Customer: Hicom Teck See
2005
Perodua Myvi Door Trim
⌄
First door trim compound programme — the Perodua Myvi (FL) door trim supply via Hicom Teck See. Marked CCSB's expansion from exterior bumper compounds into interior trim applications.
Customer: Hicom Teck See
2006
Proton Satria IP & Naza Ria Bumper
⌄
Added instrument panel (IP) compound to CCSB's product range through the Proton Satria programme with AHP, alongside bumper compound supply for the Naza Ria via TSP (SA). First IP programme milestone.
Customers: AHP / TSP (SA)
2007
Perodua MYVI FL & VIVA SE — Bumper & Door Trim
⌄
Compound supply for Perodua MYVI FL bumper and door trim, and Perodua VIVA SE bumper programmes through HTS and Permintex — strengthening presence across multiple Perodua platforms simultaneously.
Customers: HTS / Permintex
2009
Perodua Nautica Bumper & D20N IP & Child Part
⌄
Perodua Nautica bumper compound supply via Hicom Teck See, and IP & child part programmes for Perodua D20N through Permintex — expanding IP compound capabilities further into the Perodua platform.
Customers: Hicom Teck See / Permintex
2010
Perodua Alza Bumper & Proton Exora Bumper
⌄
Compound supply for Perodua Alza bumper via Hicom Teck See and Proton Exora bumper through TSP (SA). Multi-platform, multi-customer expansion solidifying CCSB's role as a core compound supplier across Malaysia's national car brands.
Customers: Hicom Teck See / TSP (SA)
2012
Proton Preve Door & Pillar Trim; Toyota Vios Bumper Grille
⌄
Door and pillar trim compound for the Proton Preve via Delloyd, and bumper grille compound for the Toyota Vios via AHP — CCSB's first supply into the non-national Japanese automotive segment.
Customers: Delloyd / AHP
2013
Proton Iriz Door & Pillar Trim
⌄
Door and pillar trim compound supply for the Proton Iriz via Delloyd — continued growth in the interior trim segment alongside bumper programmes.
Customer: Delloyd
2015
Proton New Perdana Bumper & Door Trim; Proton Persona & Saga Door Trim
⌄
Bumper and door trim compound for the Proton New Perdana via KTSM, and door trim compound for Proton Persona and Saga programmes through Delloyd — multiple Proton platforms served simultaneously.
Customers: KTSM / Delloyd
2016
Perodua Bezza Door Trim & IP Child Part
⌄
Door trim compound for Perodua Bezza via KTSM, and IP child part compound through Permintex — broadening CCSB's Bezza programme coverage across both interior trim and instrument panel applications.
Customers: KTSM / Permintex
2017
Perodua D20N Bumper RR & D38L Door Trim
⌄
Rear bumper compound for Perodua D20N via PDKM, and door trim compound for D38L via KTSM — steady expansion of PDKM-channel programmes ahead of the D38L full launch in 2018.
Customers: PDKM / KTSM
2018
Perodua Aruz (D38L) Bumper FR/RR — IATF 16949:2016 Certified
⌄
Front and rear bumper compound supply for the Perodua Aruz (D38L) via PDKM. Milestone year: CCSB achieved IATF 16949:2016 certification — the automotive industry's premier quality management system standard — confirming world-class production capability.
IATF 16949:2016 Certified
2020
Perodua D55L — Bumper RR/IP & Interior Trim
⌄
Rear bumper and IP compound for Perodua D55L via PDKM, and interior trim compound via Hil/AHP — CCSB's most comprehensive single-platform programme to date, covering exterior, IP, and interior trim simultaneously.
Customers: PDKM / Hil / AHP
2021
Perodua D51A — Bumper Front & IP
⌄
Front bumper and instrument panel compound supply for the Perodua D51A platform via PDKM — continued strengthening of the PDKM partnership across new Perodua model launches.
Customer: PDKM
2022
Perodua Alza 2022 (D27A) — Bumper Front & IP
⌄
Front bumper and IP compound for the all-new Perodua Alza 2022 (D27A) via PDKM — the highest-volume Perodua MPV launch of the decade, affirming CCSB's position as a preferred compound partner for flagship models.
Customer: PDKM
2023
Perodua Axia 2023 (D74A) — Bumper, IP & Door Trim
⌄
Full-platform compound supply for the new Perodua Axia (D74A) — front bumper and IP via PDKM, and door trim via KTSM. One of CCSB's broadest single-model coverages, spanning exterior, IP, and interior trim in one launch.
Customers: PDKM / KTSM
2025
Perodua D66B — Bumper Front, IP & Door Trim
⌄
Latest Perodua D66B platform — front bumper, IP and door trim compound supply via PDKM and KTSM. Ongoing active programme as of 2025, continuing CCSB's unbroken run as a Perodua compound partner across successive model generations.
Customers: PDKM / KTSM
2026 →
Supplier of Choice — Compound, Trading & Services
⌄
CCSB's strategic vision from 2026 onwards: to be the Supplier of Choice in Compound, Trading and Services — expanding capabilities beyond automotive into broader industrial sectors while deepening existing partnerships with Tier 1 customers across Malaysia.
