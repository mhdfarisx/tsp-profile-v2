Company Overview

Polymer Compounding &
Colouring Specialist

Established in April 1994 as a subsidiary of Teck See Plastic Group under Oriental Holdings Berhad, CCSB specialises in customised polymer compounding and colouring for the automotive, home appliances, E&E and water system industries.

With 90% of its product range manufactured from polypropylene (PP), CCSB supplies precision-formulated compounds directly to Perodua, Proton and Toyota platforms — from bumpers and door trims to instrument panels.
