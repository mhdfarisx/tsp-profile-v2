Core Business

What We Do

01
Tailor-Made Own Product

Custom polymer compounds designed specifically for automotive manufacturers. Each formulation is engineered to meet OEM technical standards — Perodua, Proton, Toyota. Products include interior trim compounds, bumper compounds and instrument panel grades, all formulated to precise mechanical and aesthetic specifications.

02
Toll Compounding & Colouring

Contract compounding and colour development for external plastic processors. CCSB provides full colour matching services with precise batch-to-batch consistency, enabling customers to outsource their compounding needs without compromising on quality or specification compliance.

03
Masterbatch

Concentrated colour and additive masterbatches for a wide range of polymer applications. Available additives include UV stabilisers, antioxidants, antistatic agents and processing aids — allowing customers to achieve precise functional properties in their final products.

04
Material Repelletising

Re-engineering and repelletising of returned or off-specification materials. CCSB's repelletising service extends material lifecycle, reduces production waste, and enables customers to recover value from reject or surplus material streams — aligned with sustainable manufacturing principles.

Material Base
90% of CCSB's product range is manufactured from polypropylene (PP). All compound grades comply with EV requirements — future-ready for electric vehicle manufacturing platforms.
