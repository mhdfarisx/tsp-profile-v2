What We Do

Full Value Chain Services

Polymer Compounding
In-house PP, ABS and PE compound formulation including masterbatches and custom colour matching for automotive and industrial applications. (Compounding & Colouring)
Mould Design & Fabrication
Precision mould design, tooling and maintenance for complex plastic components across automotive and electronics industries. (Lipro Mold Engineering)
Product Design & Prototyping
Concept design, engineering analysis and prototype development — from initial brief through to production-ready specifications. (Oriental Nichinan)
Plastic Injection Moulding
High-volume plastic injection moulding across a broad range of polymers and part sizes — serving automotive OEMs, home appliance and electronics manufacturers.
Assembly & Sub-Assembly
Component assembly and sub-assembly services for customers requiring ready-to-fit parts — reducing supply chain complexity and lead times.
Injection Moulding
Precision plastic injection moulding — producing components to spec for automotive, industrial, and consumer product applications. (Armstrong Industries)
