Message from the Managing Director

Building Excellence,
One Step at a Time

On behalf of the Board of Directors and the entire Teck See Plastic Group, it is my honour to present this corporate profile — a testament to over four decades of commitment, growth, and excellence in plastic manufacturing.

Since our establishment in 1984, Teck See Plastic has continuously evolved to meet the demands of an ever-changing global marketplace. What began as a focused plastic injection moulding operation has grown into a comprehensive group of six integrated companies, covering the full spectrum of the plastic manufacturing value chain.

Our success is built on three foundations: the trust of our customers, the dedication of our people, and our unwavering commitment to quality. These principles have guided us through decades of industry change and will continue to drive us forward.

As part of Oriental Holdings Berhad, we carry the responsibility of upholding the group's legacy of integrity and world-class standards. We remain committed to delivering value to our customers, our shareholders, and the communities in which we operate.

I invite you to explore our capabilities and look forward to the opportunity of serving your needs.

Albert Lim How Ghee
Managing Director, Teck See Plastic Group of Companies
